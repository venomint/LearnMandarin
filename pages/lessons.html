Greetings

In this lessons we learned about the most basic phrases people could say. Before we can go into these phrases we must learn how people can actually recognize the difference between pinyin and hanzi. Pinyin is the romanized version of the hanzi, which are the characters you may already know. In pinyin, there are four differen tone markers you can easy recognize.
